Sell your products online

10 Likes 2 Comments

Firstly, there is no shortage of places to sell your product. The difficulty is choosing which ones suit your brand and will enhance the offering you may or may not already have. When choosing a physical shop presence, your first thought would be to find the location best for attracting potential customers. The same is true for online.

Perhaps you started out with a local shop, but know that you could have a much wider audience. Or you already have an online presence but want to explore where else your product could be seen. Whether you’re self-taught, a novice or just curious, this guide will give you a comprehensive overview of where to sell your physical or digital product online and the many channels available to you. Take a look here if you are looking for other ways to make money online.

If you want to set up a full website where people can come to view your products and your products only (as opposed to a marketplace such as Amazon or eBay), then you’ll need to choose between a hosted or self-hosted ecommerce platform.

9 Easy Step to Sell Online In India

            1> Understand your cost structure and profit viability
            2> Prepare your digital catalog
            3> Buy a domain name
            4> Prepare your online presence
            5> Packaging process
            6> Backend software
            7> Shipper contracts
            8> Try slow and steady
            9> Scale

Picking the right ecommerce platform is just as important as the product you sell. Make sure you choose a fully hosted, all-in-one solution that allows you to own both the ecommerce website and the shopping cart software. This way, you don’t need coding skills or have to pay transaction fees on sales. You also have access to a support team to help you set up your store.
